The problem with every deposition

Attorneys walk out of depositions having captured testimony, but without the time to fully mine what they got.

✕

Contradictions buried in 300+ pages

A witness says one thing on page 47 and the opposite on page 218. Nobody catches it until trial prep — if they catch it at all.

⚠

Missed follow-ups you will not get back

The witness opened a door. The attorney moved on. That moment is gone — unless someone reviews the transcript line by line.

⏱

Trial prep takes weeks, not days

A seasoned litigator needs hours to fully analyze a single deposition. Multiply that across every witness in the case.

What Depo Hindsight delivers

A comprehensive strategy report covering every angle — with exact page:line citations back to the transcript.

Critical

Contradictions

Scans the entire transcript for statements that conflict with each other — even hundreds of pages apart. Each contradiction includes exact testimony and how to use it at trial.

Example finding
Page 47: "I never spoke to the contractor before the inspection."
Page 218: "After my phone call with the contractor, I decided to..."
[47:14] vs [218:6]
Behavioral

Evasion patterns

Flags every non-answer, redirect, and memory claim the attorney accepted and moved on from. Includes follow-up questions that would pin the witness down.

Example finding
Q: "How long were you at the site?"
A: "I was there for what I felt was an appropriate amount of time."
Pin-down: "Was it one hour or four hours?"
Strategic

Commitment map

Identifies every firm position the witness locked themselves into — and exactly how each commitment could be used against them at trial.

Example finding
Locked in: "There is absolutely no other possible cause."
Vulnerability: Contradicted by the alternative analysis in Exhibit D
Technique

Questioning technique review

Identifies compound questions, leading questions at the wrong time, and questions that let the witness escape. Provides stronger rewrites for each one.

Example finding
Original: "Did you review the documents and photos before writing your report?"
Issue: Compound — witness answered about documents only, dodged photos
Opportunity

Missed follow-ups

Doors that were opened but never walked through — volunteered information that was not pursued, partial admissions that were not expanded.

Example finding
Witness volunteered: "Well, other than that one time..."
Never asked: What was the "one time"? When? Who else was involved?
Trial prep

Cross-examination ammunition

Testimony ready to use at trial. Each item includes the exact quote, how to deploy it during cross, and setup questions to lay the foundation.

Example finding
Testimony: "I only relied on my visual inspection."
At trial: Establish industry standards require testing, then impeach with this admission
Assessment

Credibility assessment

Behavioral pattern analysis across the entire deposition — evasive, defensive, inconsistent, vague, or cooperative. Each observation is cited and categorized.

Coverage

Topic gaps

Areas that should have been explored based on the testimony but were never asked about. Includes suggested questions for a follow-up deposition or trial.
